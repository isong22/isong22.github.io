Mountain Railway - trad


Life is like a mountain railway, With an engineer that's brave;
We must make the run successful, From the cradle to the grave;
Watch the curves, the fills, the tunnels; Never falter, never quail;
Keep your hands upon the throttle, And your eyes upon the rail. 

Chorus:
Blessed Savior, Thou wilt guide us,
Till we reach that blissful shore,
When the Angels wait to join us
In your grace for evermore. 

We roll up the grades of trial; We will cross the bridge of strife;
See that God is your conductor;  On this mighty train of life;
Always mindful of obstruction, Do your duty, never fail;
Keep your hands upon the throttle, And your eyes upon the rail.
