China Cat Sunflower

Look, for awhile, at the China Cat Sunflower
Proud-walking jingle, in the midnight sun
Copperdome Bodhi drip a, silver kimono like a
Crazy-quilt stargown through a dream night wind

Krazy Kat peeking through a lace bandana like a
one-eyed Cheshire like a diamond-eye Jack
A leaf of all colors plays a golden string fiddle to a
double waterfall over my back

Comic book colors on a violin river crying
Leonardo words from out a silk trombone
I rang a silent bell beneath a shower of pearls in the
eagle wing palace of the Queen Chinee
